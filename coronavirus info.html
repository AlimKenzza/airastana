Coronavirus: Latest Updates

We care about the safety of each passenger. We need your help. Watch our video on how to protect your health during the flight. Let's combine our efforts to travel with confidence.


In the light of restrictions that have been introduced, Air Astana and FlyArystan will continue to operate international repatriation flights as far as possible in order to repatriate and relocate Kazakhstani citizens to their point of domicile. The flights list will be updated on a regular basis.


All domestic services will be suspended as of March 22nd 00:00 (midnight, March 21st).


As the situation is complicated, all passengers must be at the airport minimum of 2 hours before departure.


If you are not a resident of Kazakhstan, we kindly request you not to come to the airport as you are not allowed to enter Kazakhstan due to government restrictions.


Ticketing Policy in Response to Coronavirus

A number of our routes have been suspended and a number of passengers are unable to travel due to the government restrictions taken in response to the spread of coronavirus. We fully understand that some of your travel plans might have been interrupted as well, so to provide you with more flexibility we offer you the options specified below.

Date or destination changes without penalty across all routes

If you are travelling on any Air Astana operated route (domestic and international) and would like to either change dates or destination, you can do so:
  1. Date change (once) without penalty (collecting or refunding any fare difference, if applicable);

  2. Re-route (once) to alternative routes operated by Air Astana without penalty (collecting or refunding fare difference, if applicable).

This option applies for travel starting before the end of December 2020. We kindly request you to make the changes before 31 May 2020 and not later than 48 hours before original departure date.
