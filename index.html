Nomad Club

On the wave of change

From June 01st 2015, Nomad Club has rolled out the first phase of extended benefits for its members aiming to make your loyalty more rewarding. We are happy to announce that all structural airfares (except promotional fares in M and O classes) are now eligible for Nomad Club award and status points. With these changes introduced you will be able to achieve Silver or Gold status more quickly, as well as earn more points to obtain award tickets or upgrades.

Free Wi-Fi

Free internet access is one of many advantages offered to our Diamond members. You can easily connect to free Wi-Fi indicating your Diamond membership and seat number.

25 kg Additional Baggage Allowance

Enjoy the privilege of an additional 25 kg free baggage allowance on all domestic and international flights.

Nomad Club Points

There are two types of Nomad Club points: Flight Points and Bonus Points. Flight Points are earned on Air Astana flights and always count towards your Nomad Club Silver, Gold and Diamond qualifications. Bonus Points are usually earned for partner activities. Bonus Points contribute to your Total Points balance and can therefore be redeemed for awards.
